About Me

Aria Li, born and raised in Shenzhen, China, graduated from the USC School of Cinematic Arts with a BFA in Film & Television Production in 2024. While at USC, she interned for Janet Yang, the current president of the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences. Currently, Aria is a creative director at a public company in China, where she focuses on character and storyline design.
